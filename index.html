Projects

Two-stage Flight Delay Prediction

Flight Delays are a major operational problem for Airline corporations. They lead to critical economic repercussions. In this Project we take the arrival delay due to weather conditions of the flight. We use a two-stage predictive model where first stage is classification to classify the occurrence of flight delays. The second stage involves regression to predict the value of delay in minutes of the classified flights. The data comprises of weather data at 15 different airports and the flight data of all the flights that flew inside the USA between 2016 and 2017.
